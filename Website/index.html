Welcome

Hello and welcome to our website for Project 2! We are Niko Bessinas, Chris Thomas, and Junior Luna. For this project we took data obtained on the Divvy Bike Share program here in Chicago and visually analyzed it according to different parameters and specifications about the city.

You may use the navigation bar above to switch between the application itself as well as an information page on how we went about everything and a screenshots and videos page also highlighting our process.
